Service I am a huge advocate in passing back kindness, and am always ready to give back to my community in any way that I can. Below are a few examples of my highlighted extracurricular activities, volunteer and leadership roles. These activites have made an impact on my life, and I aim to give back to the community and youth to the best of my ability.





❮ ❯

Table Tennis

2011 - present

───

I began playing competitive table tennis when I was 10 years old under my dad's encouragement. In college, I am a part of the Texas A&M Collegiate team and officer for the Texas A&M Table Tennis Club, where I help supervise weekly practices, manage the club finances, and coach newer members. In the Spring 2022, our school did an award sweep for the National Collegiate Table Tennis (NCTTA) South Texas Divisionals Team & Singles Events, and as well as directly qualified for the Collegiate Championships.

❮ ❯

Lion dance

2011 - present

───

In Vietnamese culture, lion dancing is recognized as a symbol of luck for the Lunar New Year and special occasions. Within the Huyen Quang Buddhist Youth Association, a non-profit organization, I have volunteered and performed at countless businesses, schools, and events throughout the Houston, TX community in over 10 years. In 2020, I formed and led the first lion dance act for the National Chinese Honor Society (NCHS) at Dulles High School for our Multicultural Show that left a precedent where a performance was put on under NCHS each year since.

❮ ❯

Huyen Quang Youth Association

2009 - present

───

HQ is an organization aimed to teach youths to understand the teachings of Buddhism and to be valuable contributors in their community. As a troop leader, I now assist in leading weekly activities for +50 youths, like leading educational games, family gatherings and camping trips, and serving meals.

❮ ❯

Huyen Quang School of Vietnamese

2006 - present

───

As an Asian American, I value and embrace preserving my culture, and hope to encourage other youths to do the same. After completing Vietnamese school from 2006-2018, I volunteered in weekly Sunday classes to teach Vietnamese to classroom of thirty 8-10 year old elementary students and help with miscellaneous tasks in the principal's office.
